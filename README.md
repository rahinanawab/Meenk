# Meenk

# Location-Based Social Media Platform

# 🌍 Discover, Share & Connect Around You

**A real-time social platform built for location-based experiences and community engagement.**

Location-Based Social Media Platform enables users to discover and share content based on their location in real time. Users can explore nearby posts, upload their own content, interact with the community, and build meaningful connections through an engaging and interactive social experience.

## ✨ Features

- 📍 Location-based content discovery
- 📸 Create and share posts in real time
- ❤️ Like, comment, and share content
- 📰 News and ads integration
- 🎉 Create and participate in events
- 💬 Event-based chat functionality
- 💬 One-to-one and group chat support
- 🤝 Connection request management
- 👥 Build and manage user networks
- 🔒 Age-restricted environment for users 18+
- 📝 Waitlist and survey management
- 🖥️ Admin panel for platform management
- 🌐 Landing page for user onboarding
- 📱 Clean, modern, and user-friendly interface

## 📱 About the Platform

This location-based social platform is designed to bring communities closer by allowing users to discover what is happening around them. Users can explore nearby content, share experiences, interact with others, and participate in local events through a single connected platform.

The application provides a safe and controlled environment for users aged 18 and above while offering multiple ways to communicate, engage, and build connections.

The platform supports:

- Real-time community interactions
- Location-based content sharing
- Event discovery and participation
- Social networking
- Private and group communication
- User engagement management

## 🚀 How It Works

1. Create an account and complete onboarding.
2. Explore nearby posts and community content.
3. Upload and share location-based updates.
4. Engage with content through likes, comments, and shares.
5. Send and manage connection requests.
6. Discover events, join activities, and participate in event chats.
7. Communicate through direct and group messaging.

## 🛠️ Admin Panel Features

The admin panel provides tools for managing and monitoring the platform, including:

- 👥 User management
- 📝 Waitlist management
- 📊 Survey creation and tracking
- 📢 News and advertisement management
- 🎉 Event management
- ⚙️ Platform configuration and monitoring

## 🎯 Why Use This Platform?

- Discover content happening around you
- Connect with your local community
- Share experiences in real time
- Participate in events and discussions
- Build meaningful connections
- Enjoy a personalized social experience
